A Perfect Start to Your Monday Morning

A classic combination of traditional and modern sweet foods, these anpan red bean paste filled buns are a must-try. Anpan is one of the most beloved sweet bread buns in the country, as the thick texture of the red bean paste contrasts beautifully with the light fluffiness of the bread roll. This recipe makes the job of crafting anpan easy in a home kitchen. Enjoy for a quick breakfast, or for morning or afternoon tea.
